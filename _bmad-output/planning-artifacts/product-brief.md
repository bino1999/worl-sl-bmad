# Product Brief: Local Day Work/Job Marketplace

*This document will be collaboratively built step by step as we clarify your product vision, goals, and requirements.*

## Product Vision

**Main Problem Solved:**
- Enables both job seekers and employers to find jobs and workers quickly.

**Unique Value Proposition:**
- First platform of its kind in the Sri Lankan market—no existing application offers this service locally.

## Target Users & Key Interactions

**Job Seekers:**
- Register on the platform
- Search for jobs
- Apply to jobs

**Employers & Agencies:**
- Publish job postings

## Success Metrics
- Number of jobs posted per month
- Average time to fill a job
- Number of active job seekers and employers
- User satisfaction ratings (job seekers and employers)
- Repeat usage rate (users returning to post/apply again)

## Expanded User Personas

**Job Seekers:**
- Motivations: Find work quickly, earn income, flexible opportunities
- Needs: Easy registration, clear job details, fast application process
- Pain Points: Lack of local job options, slow response from employers, unclear pay

**Employers & Agencies:**
- Motivations: Fill positions quickly, find reliable workers, reduce hiring hassle
- Needs: Simple job posting, ability to review applicants, communication tools
- Pain Points: No-shows, unqualified applicants, time-consuming hiring

## User Journeys

**Job Seeker Journey:**
1. Register and create a profile
2. Search/filter jobs by location, skill, pay
3. Apply to jobs and communicate with employers
4. Accept job offer and confirm details

**Employer/Agency Journey:**
1. Register and create a company profile
2. Post a job with details (role, location, pay)
3. Review applicants and communicate with candidates
4. Select a worker and finalize arrangements

## Core Features
- Mobile-friendly web app
- Job posting and search
- In-app chat for negotiation and questions
- Application process (workers state availability, expected pay)
- Location-based matching
- Reviews/ratings for workers and employers
- Notifications for new jobs and applications

## Risks & Challenges
- Market adoption: Getting enough users on both sides
- Trust and reliability: Ensuring quality and reducing no-shows
- Technical: Platform stability and data privacy
- Regulatory: Compliance with local labor laws

## Business Goals
- Become the leading local platform for day work in Sri Lanka
- Achieve 1,000+ active users in the first year
- Build partnerships with local agencies and businesses
- Generate revenue through premium listings or subscription plans
